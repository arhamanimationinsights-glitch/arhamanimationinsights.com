Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

▼

We accept all major payment methods including credit cards, bank transfers, PayPal, and Stripe. A 50% deposit is required to begin the project, with final payment due upon completion. Flexible payment plans available for larger projects.

How long does a typical project take?

▼

Project duration varies based on complexity. Simple product animations typically take 2-4 weeks, while complex projects with multiple shots can take 6-8 weeks or more. We provide detailed timelines during the quote phase.

What is your revisions policy?

▼

We include up to 3 rounds of revisions in our standard packages. Each revision should include specific feedback. Additional revisions available at hourly rates. We ensure complete satisfaction before project completion.

What is your delivery process?

▼

We deliver animations in multiple formats (MP4, MOV, WebM) optimized for different platforms. All files provided in high resolution (up to 4K) and ready for immediate use across marketing channels, websites, and social media.
